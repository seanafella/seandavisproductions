About Me

As a dedicated teacher and facilitator, my core values revolve around facilitating the learning of others, whether through direct student engagement or department-wide collaboration. I am deeply inspired by the power of collaboration and believe it is the key to effective learning and growth.

I am PGCE certified for both secondary and primary education, and I am a recently qualified EduScrum Certified Student. This certification has empowered me to integrate core LEAN values into my professional development and enhance my ability to foster a collaborative and efficient learning environment.

With a First Class Honour BSc in Audio Engineering, I bring a technological perspective to my teaching methods. This background has paved the way for my role as Head of Computing, where I continuously seek innovative and adaptive approaches to teaching and team facilitation.

I also coordinate STEAM projects across different departments, enabling students to develop critical thinking and collaboration skills. These skills are essential for effective work both in an educational sense and a professional environment, preparing students for success beyond the classroom.

My hobbies and interests include staying updated with technological trends, particularly in accessibility within education and music. I am passionate about exploring new ways to enhance learning experiences and facilitate effective teamwork.
